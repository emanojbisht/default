Camping

Nanital

Nainital is one of the best hill stations in India that is also famous for camping. Tourists from all over the world visit the place for adventure camping in Uttarakhand. Its pear-shaped lake and scenic beauty continue to be the attraction point for travelers worldwide. Accommodation is available in camps and tents that let you feel closer to nature. There are several activities other than camping that you can participate in, such as trekking, rock climbing, paragliding, hiking, and so on. The adventure sports in Nainital are available in a budget that would cater to everybody..

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is another best camping places in Uttarakhand, which is located in the Himalayan foothills. This city is a sacred one located in the Dehradun district and is also a popular pilgrimage hub. Every year thousands of pilgrims visit the place due to its importance as a spiritual hub. The place offers a soothing environment where you can connect with nature. Enjoy rafting in the waters of the Ganges along with varied other adventure sports. Plan your next camp in Rishikesh with your friends and family to fill your soul with contentment.

Corbett

Corbett is one of the several popular national parks in India which offers accommodation inside the reserve. There are several stay options to choose from thanks to the ecotourism zones making it a highly popular camping place. You can not only reside in luxurious safari-style rents but also in cottage camps. While soaking in nature, you can take a safari through the tiger or even indulge yourself on an exciting elephant safari. Corbett is one of the best camping places in Uttarakhand that allows you to explore the wildlife population while staying close to them. Indulge your free time in bird watching and make most of the beauty that surrounds this camping area.

Auli

Auli is located at the height of 8,200 ft is a popular tourist spot located in the Chamoli district. The place is situated at the height of 8,200 feet in the Himalayas and attracts tourists largely for camping. Apart from camping, the snow clad mountains surrounding also make it a popular ski destination among adventure seekers. This is a meadow that is surrounded by coniferous and oak trees while offering a breathtaking view of the Himalayas. Camping offers a breathtaking experience, especially due to the ambiance it is set in. You can also indulge yourself in night walks in the jungle to have a thrilling experience.
